Reflections

I began my HTML journey on freeCodeCamp as an initiative to enhance our HTML skills independently, a task assigned by our lecturer. Initially, it was enjoyable and motivating, but the pressure to complete the tasks within a strict timeline soon made it overwhelming. For many of us, including myself, the workload felt excessive, especially since we had other courses to manage as well. Despite the challenges, I understand that our lecturer has our best interests at heart, and that thought keeps me moving forward—for now. However, I wish he could ease up on the deadlines, as the pressure makes me focus on simply completing the tasks rather than truly improving my web development skills. Challenges and Triumphs

Although the pressure can be overwhelming, I still feel a sense of accomplishment when I complete a challenge or create a project. FreeCodeCamp has been an excellent learning platform, but the external demands sometimes take away from the enjoyment of the process. My goal is to hold onto these skills and use them effectively in real-world applications.
